# PIZZA-SALES-ANALYSIS
## Objective
The objective of this dataset is to analyze pizza sales transactions to understand overall business performance.
It aims to evaluate total revenue, orders, and product demand patterns.
The analysis helps identify best-selling and least-selling pizzas along with peak sales hours.
The insights support data-driven decisions for inventory, pricing, and operational improvement.

## KPI'S
1. Total Revenue – Total income generated from pizza sales.
2. Total Orders – Total number of customer orders placed.
3. Total Pizzas Sold – Total quantity of pizzas sold.
4. Average Order Value (AOV) – Average revenue generated per order.
5. Average Pizzas per Order – Average number of pizzas purchased in each order.
6.Top-Selling Pizza (by Quantity) – Pizza with the highest sales volume.
7. Lowest-Selling Pizza – Pizza with the lowest sales volume.
8. Revenue by Category – Total revenue generated by each pizza category (Classic, Veggie, Chicken, Supreme).
9. Sales by Pizza Size – Performance comparison across sizes (S, M, L, XL, XXL).
10.Peak Sales Hour – Hour of the day with the highest number of orders.
